AJAX Pauka

The AJAX Spider add-on integrates in ZAP a crawler of AJAX rich sites called Crawljax. Možete ga koristiti za identifikaciju stranica ciljanog sajta. You can combine it with the (normal) spider for better results.

The spider is configured using the Options AJAX Spider screen.

Dostupan preko

     AJAX Spider taba
     History tab 'Attack/AJAX Spider...' right click menu item
     Sajtovi taba 'Attack/AJAX Spider...' right click menu item
